Carian Sorcery

Carian Sorcery is a specialized branch of magic associated with the Carian Royal Family and their loyal knights. These sorceries are characterized by their use of enchanted weaponry and defensive magic, reflecting the martial traditions and arcane knowledge of the Carian lineage.

These sorceries blend physical and magical combat, making them versatile tools for both offensive and defensive strategies. Carian Sorceries often require investment in the Intelligence stat and taught by NPCs with connections to this prestigious lineage. Overall, Carian Sorcery offers players a balanced approach to magic in Elden Ring, combining the elegance and power of traditional sorcery with the practical combat skills of a knight. This makes them an excellent choice for players who enjoy a hybrid playstyle, utilizing both melee and magical attacks.

Modules

Carian Greatsword

Conjures a magic greatsword and then delivers a sweeping blow. This sorcery can be cast repeatedly. Armed with this spell, sorcerers gain the strength of knights, their loyalty sworn to the moon.

Carian Phalanx

Creates a defensive arch of numerous magic glintblades overhead, which automatically attack nearby foes. This sorcery can be used cast while in motion. An enhanced version of the glintblade sorcery used by enchanted knights.


Carian Piercer

Conjures a magic greatsword to impale foes. Charging lengthens the initial step in. This sorcery forms a counterpart to Carian Greatsword. Highly lethal when used against single foes riding alone.


Carian Retaliation

Swing your staff to dispel incoming sorceries and incantations, using their power to retaliate with glintblades. This was the Carian royal family's secret means to prepare against the disloyalty of the academy. The moon and stars would one day go their separate ways.


Carian Slicer

Conjures a magic sword and delivers a swift sweeping slash. This sorcery can be used without delay after performing another action. Even among the Carian sword sorceries, this is a notably effective technique.


Glintblade Phalanx

Creates a defensive arch of magic glintblades overhead, which automatically attack nearby foes. This sorcery can be cast while in motion. Bestowed upon the enchanted Carian knights, who combined this art with their swordsmanship to maintain pressure upon their foes, striking in waves of steel and sorcery.


Greatblade Phalanx

Creates a defensive arch of larger magic glintblades overhead, which automatically attack nearby foes. Used by the enchanted troll knights. They were the comrades of the young Rennala, bound by oath.


Lucidity

Alleviates buildup of sleep and madness. This sorcery can be cast while in motion. The Carian knights never waver.


Magic Downpour

Summons a mass of magic that fires projectiles over a wide area. This sorcery can be cast while in motion. Charging extends duration. Said to have been taught by the Crystalians to mark the swearing of the old concord.


Magic Glintblade

An old sorcery of the Carian Royal Family. Creates a sigil overhead, from which an enemy-seeking glintblade appears after a brief delay. This sorcery can be used while in motion. Charging increases the delay. Said to be the prototype of the sword-phalanx sorceries wielded by royal enchanted knights.


Miriam's Vanishing

Sorcery of a preceptor who served the Carian royal family. Used to conceal oneself in a glintstone haze, reappearing after a few seconds have passed. Directional inputs determine where one will reappear. The curator of the study hall was such a master of this sorcery that it now bears her name.


Glintblade Trio

An old sorcery of the Carian royal family. Creates a sigil overhead, from which three enemy-seeking glintblades appear after a brief delay. This sorcery can be used while in motion. Charging increases the delay. The prototypical form of sword-phalanx sorceries saw a different subsequent refinement in the realm of shadow.
